Welcome to Minesi.de!

What our server is:

Minesi.de is a chill vanilla survival server where you can build, explore, and be part of a lasting community. Whether you're into massive construction projects, redstone engineering, resource gathering, or just vibing with friends in a persistent world, you'll find your place here. We keep gameplay pure vanilla with a few helpful additions that make collaboration smoother without changing the core experience.

Info:

The server runs on dedicated hardware with daily backups and optimized performance. We use the latest Java Edition and maintain a large persistent world with room for everyone's projects. Everything is player-made, from the shopping districts to the community farms.

The server is whitelisted to maintain our tight-knit community atmosphere. We're not trying to be the biggest server out there. We keep things small so everyone knows each other, builds trust, and creates genuine friendships. No land claiming systems, no economy plugins, no teleportation commands. Just pure vanilla Minecraft.

You can join the Minecraft server at minesi.de after completing the whitelist application on our Discord.

What Makes Us Different:

Long-Term World: Everything you build stays. No resets, no wipes. Your base, your farms, your megaprojects are all permanent. The world evolves as players add to it over time.

True Vanilla Feel: We don't add plugins that change gameplay. Just Minecraft as it was meant to be played, with backend improvements for stability and performance.

Player-Driven Experience: No admin-run shops or spawn builds. Want a shopping district? Build one. Want community farms? Organize it with others. The world is shaped entirely by its players.
